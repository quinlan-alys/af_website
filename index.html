Bio

Beneath a gentle, good-humoured exterior lies a melancholy darkness that emerges softly from her lyrics and tone. Avery Florence is a soft-soul artist whose talent as a songwriter is matched with her angelic yet gritty voice, and undeniable personality - soulful & inspiring.
